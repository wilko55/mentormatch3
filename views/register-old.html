Rules of engagement

Be polite, punctual and considerate. This is a professional relationship and both parties should approach it with the professionalism it deserves.

We suggest that mentors/ mentees start out by meeting for an hour or so a month and take things from there. For more information about basic mentoring / coaching frameworks, click here.

Don’t pay and don’t ask for payment. This includes poorly-veiled requests for bribes. If you want to make money, you can ask google about becoming a life coach (seriously, do this, it’s hilarious).

Don’t ask your mentor/ mentee/ coach for a job. If someone asks you for a job, we recommend subtly directing them to CSJ.
